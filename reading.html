Computational Cognitive Science Reading Group

This is the webpage of the Computational Cognitive Science reading group. It's an informal reading group for people interested in the computational aspects of cognitive science, as well as the intersection between cognitive science and artificial intelligence.

We meet every other Wednesday at 3pm on the third floor of the Bob and Betty Beyster building. If you are interested in presenting any work for the reading group or you would like to be added to the mailing list, please contact Mohamed El Banani.
